Site Proposition: Dice & Gather

Dice & Gather is a platform designed to connect tabletop gamers, helping them find groups and access game materials. The site caters to people seeking new gaming experiences, whether they're new to a city or need a substitute player for a canceled game. It offers features like group search, resources for game preparation, and community building. The name combines two key aspects of tabletop gaming—*Dice* and *Gather*—reflecting the site’s mission to bring people together to play, roll dice, and have fun. It's perfect for gamers looking for a fun, easy way to connect.
