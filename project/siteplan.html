Site Name

Dice & Gather. is a great name for a site that helps people find gaming groups because it cleverly combines two key aspects of tabletop gaming: Dice and Gather.The name is catchy, easy to remember, and clearly conveys the purpose of the site—bringing gamers together to roll dice and have fun!

Site Purpose

The sites provides a plataform for people to find games and people to play with. Along with the material for playing.

Scenarios

Someone just moved to a new city and is eager to find a Dungeons & Dragons group. Or someone's weekly board game night is in trouble—one of their regulars canceled at the last minute.

Color Schema

Deep Red (#8B0000) – Evokes classic RPG books and dice sets. Gold (#FFD700) – Feels prestigious and welcoming. Dark Brown (#3E2723) – Aged parchment/map aesthetic. Cream (#FAF3E0) – A warm, neutral background.

Typography

Header Font: Uncial Antiqua or Cinzel – Medieval, fantasy-style fonts that feel like they belong in an RPG rulebook. Body Font: Merriweather or Lora – Clean, readable serif fonts that complement a fantasy theme.

Wireframe

Navigation Bar: (Top) Logo (Left) Links: Home, Find a Game, Host a Game, Community, About Login/Sign-up Button (Right) Hero Section: (Main Banner) Big heading: "Find Your Next Gaming Group!" Subtext: "Discover players near you or host your own game—RPGs, board games, and more!" Search Bar: Location & Game Type filter Call-to-Action Button: "Find a Game!" Featured Games Section: Grid of game categories (D&D, Catan, Magic: The Gathering, Warhammer, etc.) Clicking a category filters results Game Listings Section: Cards with details: Game Name, Host, Location (or Online), Player Slots Available Filter & Sort Options Community & Reviews Section: Testimonials from happy players Forum highlights or Discord link Footer: Social links Contact info Quick links to FAQ, Terms, and Privacy
